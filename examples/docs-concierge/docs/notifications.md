# Notifications

## Channels

Skylark sends notifications in-app and by email. Each member controls both
channels separately from Personal Settings; admins cannot change another
member's notification preferences.

## The daily digest

The email digest summarizes activity across your workspaces once a day. Pick
the delivery hour in Personal Settings, or switch to weekly. Mentions and
direct assignments always send immediately regardless of digest settings.

## Muting a board

Mute any board from its menu to stop all notifications from it except direct
mentions. Muting is personal; it never affects other members.
