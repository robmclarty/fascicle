# Exports and data retention

## Exporting a board

Admins and editors can export any board to CSV from the board menu. The
export includes task titles, assignees, labels, due dates, and comments.
Viewer accounts cannot export.

## Workspace export

Admins can request a full workspace export from Workspace Settings. It
arrives as a download link by email within an hour and stays valid for
7 days.

## Data retention

A deleted project stays recoverable for 30 days, then its data is removed
permanently. Deleted workspaces follow the same 30-day window. Exports are
not retained on Skylark servers after the download link expires.
